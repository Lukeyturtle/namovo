About

Namovo is a municipality inside of New Skailia. It is home to the NNEPP (Namovo Nuclear Energy Power Plant), NIA (Namovo Intelligence Agency) and the CBfANCO (Central Base for Advanced Namovo Combative Operations). It is a protected municipality so you must apply for citizenship even if you are already a citizen of New Skailia.
